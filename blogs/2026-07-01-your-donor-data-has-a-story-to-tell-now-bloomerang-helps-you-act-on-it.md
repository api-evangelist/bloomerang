Bloomerang has partnered with Dataro to integrate predictive donor intelligence into its giving platform, enabling fundraising teams to identify which donors need attention and receive AI-powered recommendations for next steps. The integration combines Dataro's predictive analytics with Bloomerang's CRM and Penny AI assistant to help nonprofits prioritize outreach and personalize donor engagement.
